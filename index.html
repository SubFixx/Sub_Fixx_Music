Artist Profile

Bio (Long)

Sub Fixx is an underground electronic artist from Port Alberni, British Columbia, Canada, crafting emotionally driven bass music rooted in UK drum & bass culture. Beginning his DJ journey in 1999 through hip-hop and turntablism under the alias Faces, he spent years immersed in nightclub culture and underground events before stepping away in 2016.

In 2018, he re-emerged with a clear artistic reset, rebranding as Sub Fixx — a name derived from his signature approach to heavy sub-bass and deep emotional weight. After committing fully to drum & bass in 2023, two of the first three tracks he produced in the genre were signed, leading to over 30 original releases across drum & bass, UK garage, and bass-driven styles.

Music has been central to structuring and navigating his neurodivergence, transforming it from limitation into strength. Sub Fixx returned to the stage in January 2025 after a nine-year absence, now prioritizing professionally run underground events built on mutual respect, artist care, and sound-system culture.

Bio

Sub Fixx is a Canadian drum & bass producer creating emotionally driven underground bass music built for dark rooms, loud systems, and connection.

Rooted in UK bass culture and shaped by years of multi-genre influence, his sound blends weight, tension, and atmosphere with deep low-end pressure. Every track is designed to hit with intent — not just to move bodies, but to pull people into the moment.

The journey started in 1999 with his first set of turntables — long nights digging, learning, and building taste the hard way. After years of experimenting across sounds and scenes, he fully committed in the last couple of years when he switched to drum & bass — a move that became a true game changer and sharpened the direction into something focused, heavy, and honest.

This is music for immersive dancefloors, late nights, and shared energy.

Start here: listen on SoundCloud, then book / contact for shows, collabs, and label inquiries.

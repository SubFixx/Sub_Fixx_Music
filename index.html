Bio

Sub Fixx is a Canadian drum & bass producer creating emotionally driven underground bass music built for dark rooms, loud systems, and connection.

Rooted in UK bass culture and shaped by years of multi-genre influence, his sound blends weight, tension, and atmosphere with deep low-end pressure.

The journey started in 1999 with his first set of turntables — long nights digging, learning, and building taste the hard way.

This is music for immersive dancefloors, late nights, and shared energy.
